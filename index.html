> whoarewe

cyber673 is group of cybersecurity enthusiasts who want to share what they know and grow local capabilities when it comes to offensive & defensive cybersecurity. We are a community of hackers, security professionals, students, and anyone who is interested in cybersecurity.

Our events are powered by Swarmnetics & Nitro Security and supported by Brunei Cyber Security Association.
