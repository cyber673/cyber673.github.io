> whoarewe

cyber673 is managed by cyber673 Company, a registered partnership in Brunei Darussalam. We are a team of cybersecurity enthusiasts, professionals, and educators dedicated to sharing knowledge and growing local capabilities in offensive & defensive cybersecurity. We foster a community of hackers, security experts, students, and anyone passionate about cybersecurity.

Our community events are powered by Swarmnetics & Nitro Security and supported by Brunei Cyber Security Association.

> services
