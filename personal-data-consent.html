> Data_Consent_Details

This page provides detailed information about what you are consenting to when you check the consent checkbox on our event registration form.

> Data_Collection

By checking the consent checkbox, you agree that we may collect the following personal data through the registration form:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Organization Name

In addition, you have the *option* to upload your CV (Curriculum Vitae) or resume.

> Purpose_of_Data_Collection

The data collected above will be used for the following purposes:

  • Attendance tracking for cyber673 events and meetups.
  • Communication related to event logistics, updates, and important announcements.

CV Upload (Optional): If you choose to upload your CV, it will *also* be used to create a talent pool. This talent pool will be used *solely* to refer potential candidates to organizations who contact cyber673 seeking cybersecurity professionals. Your CV will *not* be shared without our explicit consent.

> Data_Sharing

Your personal data (Name, Email, Organization) will be shared with the following entities:

  • Entity 1: Swarmnetics - Event sponsors who help in arranging logistics and support.
  • Entity 2: Nitro Security - Event sponsors who help in arranging logistics and support.

These entities are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes described here.

CV Upload (Optional): If you upload your CV, it will *not* be directly shared with these entities. Instead, cyber673 will act as a referral service, connecting potential candidates (from the CV talent pool) with organizations seeking cybersecurity professionals.

> Data_Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above, or as required by applicable laws and regulations.

CV Upload (Optional): If you upload your CV, it will be retained in our talent pool until you request its removal (see "Your Rights" below).

> Your_Rights_and_Withdrawal_of_Consent

You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. To do so, please contact us at pdpo@cyber673.com with the subject line "Withdraw Consent". Upon receiving your request, we will stop using your data for these purposes, except where required by law.

CV Upload (Optional): If you have uploaded your CV and wish to have it removed from our talent pool, please contact us at pdpo@cyber673.com with the subject line "Remove CV".

Withdrawing your consent will not affect any processing of your data that occurred before your withdrawal, nor will it affect processing that is required by law.

> Contact_Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our data protection practices, please contact us at: pdpo@cyber673.com.

This Data Consent Details page is subject to change. We will notify you of any material changes by posting an updated page on our website.

Please return to the registration form to indicate your consent and upload your CV (optional).
